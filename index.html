About Us

Universal Mercy (UM) was incorporated in 2015 with it's first offering being a fulltime Islamic school known as Universal Mercy Academy. In 2022 we stopped the school operation due to, but not limited to, us not meeting our staffing and financial requirements for the upcoming years. While the K-5 school is closed, Universal Mercy will remain in operation with other programs under our umbrella continuing their operation.
