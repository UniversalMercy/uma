About our Islamic Homeschool Co-Op
At Universal Mercy, we strongly believe in developing the whole child, and we equip our students with essential tools that lay the foundation for becoming lifelong, confident learners with a strong sense of self.

Well rounded Islamic curriculum to produce confidence in faith.
